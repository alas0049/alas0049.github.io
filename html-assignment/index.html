About Me

"Flowers are an elegant language" Azhar

The word ‘Flowers’ has so many deep meanings in people’s lives such as Love, happiness, hope, forgiveness, and beauty. My name is Azhar, which means flowers in Arabic.
Therefore, I started introducing myself with the meaning of my name as I feel that my name describes many things about my personality.

I am 24 years old, I was born on 27 th April, 1995, originally from the country of Yemen and I have been living in this beautiful country (Canada) for four years. My passion for designing made me pursue studies in IMD despite this being a new challenge for me. Additionally, I possess the curiosity and ambition to learn and better understand the world of Interactive Media, its relation to the internet, and coding.
